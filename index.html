Keywords & tracking

Roles are matched on title, case-insensitive. One keyword per line.

Every open fetches the boards live, so everything listed is open right now. "New" means published since your previous visit (tracked on this device). "AU + US" hides roles whose location is clearly elsewhere; roles listing multiple locations are kept if any is in Australia or the US. Location text varies by company, so this is a best-effort filter — turn it off to see everything.
